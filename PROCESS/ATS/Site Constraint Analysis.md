# Site Constraint Analysis Process
部署环境分析流程

---

## 1 Purpose

Evaluate environmental and infrastructure constraints that may affect the datacenter design.

评估部署环境与基础设施条件对数据中心设计的影响。

---

## 2 Inputs

Site location  
Climate data  
Power availability  
Land availability  
Local regulations

部署地点  
气候条件  
电力条件  
场地条件  
当地法规

---

## 3 Process Steps

1 Identify available electrical capacity

2 Identify ambient temperature range

3 Evaluate cooling feasibility

4 Identify regulatory requirements

5 Evaluate logistics constraints

---

## 4 Outputs

Power availability report  
Cooling feasibility analysis  
Deployment constraints summary

电力可用性报告  
冷却可行性分析  
部署约束总结

---

## 5 Key Decisions

**Hybrid Cooling System** vs 冷却塔
**Hybrid Cooling System**配置要求（环境≥28°C时DX强制启动）
Container vs building

---

## 6 Risks

Grid instability  
Extreme climate  
Noise limitations
