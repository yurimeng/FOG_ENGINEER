冷却架构设计流程

---

## 1 Purpose

Design cooling infrastructure capable of supporting the IT load.

设计能够支持IT负载的冷却系统。

---

## 2 Inputs

IT load (must distinguish from total facility load — see [[KB/POWER_LOAD.md|KB/POWER_LOAD]])
Ambient temperature
Cooling water availability

IT负载（必须与整体电力负荷区分 — 参考 [[KB/POWER_LOAD.md|KB/POWER_LOAD]]）
环境温度
水资源条件

---

## 3 Process Steps

1 Select cooling method

2 Design heat rejection system

3 Design pipe network

4 Evaluate redundancy

---

## 4 Outputs

Cooling system architecture  
Heat rejection method  
Pipe specifications

冷却系统架构  
散热方式  
管路规格

---

## 5 Key Decisions

**Hybrid Cooling System** vs 冷却塔
**Hybrid Cooling System**配置要求（环境≥28°C时DX强制启动）

---

## 6 Risks

Insufficient cooling capacity  
High operational complexity
