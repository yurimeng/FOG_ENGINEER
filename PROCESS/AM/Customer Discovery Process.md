# Customer Discovery Process
客户需求发现流程

---

## 1 Purpose

Understand customer goals, constraints, and expectations.

理解客户目标、限制条件和期望。

---

## 2 Inputs

Qualified opportunity  
Customer meeting notes

已筛选项目机会  
客户会议记录

---

## 3 Process Steps

1 Identify business objectives

2 Identify compute requirements

3 Identify deployment location

4 Identify timeline expectations

5 Identify budget expectations

---

## 4 Outputs

Customer requirement notes

客户需求记录

---

## 5 Key Questions

What compute capacity is required?

What is the deployment timeline?

What is the available power capacity?

Is container deployment acceptable?

需要多少算力？

部署时间要求？

可用电力多少？

是否接受容器部署？
