# Lead Qualification Process
销售线索筛选流程

---

## 1 Purpose / 目的

Determine whether a potential customer opportunity should proceed to the solution design stage.

判断潜在客户机会是否值得进入方案设计阶段。

---

## 2 Inputs / 输入

Initial customer contact  
Project description  
Customer industry  
Estimated project size

客户初步接触  
项目描述  
客户行业  
项目规模估计

---

## 3 Process Steps / 流程步骤

Step 1  
Identify customer organization and decision makers.

Step 2  
Understand high-level project objective.

Step 3  
Estimate potential project scale.

Step 4  
Evaluate whether the opportunity aligns with company capabilities.

---

## 4 Outputs / 输出

Qualified opportunity  
Disqualified opportunity

合格项目机会  
不合格项目机会

---

## 5 Risks / 风险提示

Unclear customer ownership  
Projects without budget  
Unrealistic timelines
