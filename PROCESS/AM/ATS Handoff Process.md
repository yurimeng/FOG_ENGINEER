# ATS Handoff Process
ATS交接流程

---

## 1 Purpose

Transfer structured requirements from AM to ATS for solution design.

将结构化需求从 AM 交接给 ATS 进行方案设计。

---

## 2 Inputs

Customer Requirement Brief

客户需求摘要

---

## 3 Process Steps

1 Review requirement completeness

2 Confirm critical constraints

3 Transfer requirement brief to ATS

4 Schedule architecture discussion if necessary

---

## 4 Outputs

ATS design request

ATS方案设计请求

---

## 5 Risks

Incomplete requirements  
Unclear deployment constraints  
Budget mismatch
