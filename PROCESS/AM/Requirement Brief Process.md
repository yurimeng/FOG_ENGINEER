# Requirement Brief Process
需求摘要生成流程

---

## 1 Purpose

Convert discovery information into a structured requirement brief for ATS.

将客户需求转化为结构化需求文档，供 ATS 使用。

---

## 2 Inputs

Customer discovery notes

客户需求记录

---

## 3 Process Steps

1 Summarize customer information

2 Define compute scale

3 Define deployment location

4 Define timeline

5 Define budget expectations

---

## 4 Outputs

Customer Requirement Brief

客户需求摘要
