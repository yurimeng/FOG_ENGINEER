# # Account Manager (AM) / 客户经理

Document Version: v1.0
Last Updated: 2026-03-10

---

# Role Definition / 角色定义

EN

The Account Manager (AM) is responsible for managing customer relationships and translating customer needs into structured requirements.

The AM acts as the bridge between customers and the engineering team.

The AM does not design technical solutions.  
Instead, the AM ensures that customer requirements are clearly understood and properly communicated to the ATS team.

CN

客户经理（AM）负责管理客户关系，并将客户需求转化为结构化需求信息。

AM 是客户与工程团队之间的桥梁。

AM 不负责设计技术方案，而是确保客户需求被正确理解，并清晰传递给 ATS 团队。

---

# Mission / 使命

EN

Understand customer goals and translate them into structured requirements for solution design.

CN

理解客户目标，并将其转化为可用于方案设计的结构化需求。

# Boundaries / 职责边界

EN

AM is responsible for:

Customer communication  
Requirement discovery  
Commercial alignment  

AM is not responsible for:

Technical architecture design  
Power calculation  
Cooling system design  
Infrastructure engineering

CN

AM 负责：

客户沟通  
需求发现  
商业协调  

AM 不负责：

技术架构设计  
功率计算  
冷却系统设计  
基础设施工程设计
