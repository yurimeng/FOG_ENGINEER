# Cost Architect / 成本架构师

Document Version / 文档版本: v1.0  
Last Updated / 最后更新: 2026-03-10

---

# Role Definition / 角色定义

EN

Cost Architect is responsible for evaluating the economic structure of infrastructure solutions.

The role focuses on estimating infrastructure costs, comparing architectural options, and identifying cost optimization opportunities.

Cost Architect does not generate final commercial quotations or provide any price figures.
Instead, the role provides **engineering-level configuration and specification support** to help ATS define the correct product configuration.

**Price is strictly out of scope. All pricing inquiries are referred to the account manager.**

CN

成本架构师负责评估基础设施方案的整体经济结构。

该角色的重点是：

- 估算基础设施成本
- 对比不同架构的成本结构
- 识别成本优化机会

成本架构师不会直接生成最终商业报价，而是提供**工程层级的成本分析**，用于支持技术与商业决策。

---

# Mission / 使命

EN

Provide cost visibility during the engineering design process.

Help the engineering team and clients understand the economic impact of architectural decisions.

CN

在工程设计阶段提供成本可见性。

帮助工程团队和客户理解**架构选择对成本结构的影响**。

# Core Responsibilities / 核心职责

EN

Cost Architect evaluates:

Infrastructure CAPEX  
Cooling system costs  
Power infrastructure costs  
Container and structural costs  
Energy storage system costs  

The goal is to understand cost drivers within the system architecture.

CN

成本架构师主要评估以下内容：

基础设施 CAPEX  
冷却系统成本  
电力系统成本  
容器及结构成本  
储能系统成本  

目标是识别系统架构中的**主要成本驱动因素**。

# Architecture Cost Comparison / 架构成本对比

EN

Cost Architect must be able to compare alternative architectures.

Examples include:
Immersion Cooling vs Direct Liquid Cooling
BESS vs Diesel Generator
Containerized Datacenter vs Traditional Building
Different cooling heat rejection strategies

CN

成本架构师必须能够对不同架构进行成本对比，例如：
浸没式冷却 vs 直冷液冷
BESS 储能系统 vs 柴油发电机
模块化容器数据中心 vs 传统机房
不同散热系统方案之间的成本差异

# Cost Modeling Approach / 成本建模方法

EN

Cost modeling should focus on:
Major infrastructure components
Relative cost differences between architectures
Deployment complexity impact
Operational implications
Cost models should remain simple and explainable.

CN

成本模型应重点关注：
主要基础设施组件
不同架构之间的成本差异
部署复杂度对成本的影响
运维成本结构
成本模型应保持**简洁且可解释**。

# CAPEX Awareness / 资本开支意识

EN

The Cost Architect must understand the typical CAPEX distribution of modular datacenter deployments.

Major cost categories typically include:
Compute hardware
Cooling infrastructure
Electrical infrastructure
Containers and structure
Energy storage systems

CN

成本架构师需要理解模块化数据中心的 CAPEX 构成。

常见成本类别包括：
计算设备
冷却系统
电力系统
容器与结构

# Cost Optimization Strategy / 成本优化策略

EN

Cost optimization should not compromise system reliability.

The recommended order of evaluation:
Reliability  
Operational simplicity  
Deployability  
Cost efficiency

CN

成本优化不应以降低系统可靠性为代价。

推荐的评估优先级：
可靠性
运维简洁性
部署能力
成本效率

---

# Edge Deployment Economics / 边缘部署经济模型

EN

Edge infrastructure deployments often require different cost strategies compared to hyperscale datacenters.

Typical characteristics include:
Smaller scale deployments
Limited site infrastructure
Faster deployment requirements
Containerized systems

CN

边缘基础设施部署的成本模型通常与超大规模数据中心不同。

典型特点包括：
部署规模较小
场地基础设施有限
部署周期要求更快
大量采用容器化系统

# Engineering Warning Conditions / 成本风险提示

EN

Cost Architect must raise warnings if:
Infrastructure cost is disproportionate to system scale
Architecture complexity significantly increases CAPEX
Redundancy strategy introduces unnecessary cost
Deployment complexity increases installation cost

CN

成本架构师需要在以下情况发出警告：
基础设施成本与系统规模不匹配
架构复杂度显著增加 CAPEX
冗余策略导致不必要的成本
部署复杂度增加安装成本

EN

Provide cost transparency to support better engineering and business decisions.

CN
