# Engineering Workspace Workflow
工程工作流

Document Version: v1.0
Last Updated: 2026-03-10

---

# 1 Overview / 总览

EN

This document defines the operational workflow of the engineering workspace.

It specifies how different agents and processes collaborate to transform customer requirements into complete infrastructure solutions.

CN

本文档定义工程 Workspace 的整体工作流程。

它描述了不同 Agent 与 Process 如何协同，将客户需求转化为完整的基础设施方案。

---

# 2 Workflow Stages / 工作流阶段

The engineering workflow is divided into six stages.

工程工作流程分为六个阶段。

1 Lead Stage  
2 Discovery Stage  
3 Architecture Stage  
4 Engineering Stage  
5 Governance Stage  
6 Proposal Stage (Configuration only — NO price)

---

# 3 Stage 1 – Lead Qualification
销售机会筛选阶段

Responsible Agent

AM

Process

Lead Qualification Process

Goal

Determine whether the opportunity is worth pursuing.

判断该项目是否值得投入资源。

Output

Qualified Opportunity

合格项目机会

---

# 4 Stage 2 – Customer Discovery
客户需求发现阶段

Responsible Agent

AM

Process

Customer Discovery Process

Goal

Understand customer requirements and constraints.

理解客户需求和限制条件。

Output

Customer Requirement Notes

客户需求记录

---

# 5 Stage 3 – Requirement Structuring
需求结构化阶段

Responsible Agent

AM

Process

Requirement Brief Process

Goal

Convert customer discussions into a structured requirement brief.

将客户需求整理为结构化需求文档。

Output

Customer Requirement Brief

客户需求摘要

---

# 6 Stage 4 – Architecture Design
架构设计阶段

Responsible Agent

ATS

Processes

Customer Requirement Analysis  
Site Constraint Analysis  
Architecture Selection Process

Goal

Determine the optimal infrastructure architecture.

确定最优基础设施架构。

Output

Architecture Blueprint

架构蓝图

---

# 7 Stage 5 – Engineering Design
工程设计阶段

Responsible Agents

Engineering Specialists

Power Engineer  
Cooling Engineer  
Layout Planner  
Cost Architect

Processes

Capacity Planning Process  
Cooling Architecture Process  
Solution Design Process

Goal

Develop a complete engineering solution.

形成完整工程方案。

Output

Engineering Design Package

工程设计方案

---

# 8 Stage 6 – Governance Review
工程审查阶段

Responsible Agents

Compliance Officer  
Risk Auditor

Goal

Ensure the design is compliant, reliable, and maintainable.

确保方案符合规范、可靠且可运维。

Output

Approved Engineering Solution

审核通过的工程方案

---

# 9 Stage 7 – Proposal Generation
方案输出阶段

Responsible Agent

ATS

Process

Proposal Generation Process

Goal

Generate a customer-facing **configuration proposal** (no price, no quotation).

生成客户方案（仅配置，不含价格）。

Output

Customer Configuration Proposal
(IT load, total facility load, product model, PUE, redundancy)

客户技术方案
（IT负载、整体电力负荷、产品型号、PUE、冗余等级）
